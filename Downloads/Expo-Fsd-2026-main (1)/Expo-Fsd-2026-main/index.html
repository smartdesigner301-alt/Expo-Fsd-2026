ABOUT EXPO FAISALABAD

Expo Faisalabad 2026 stands as the premier gateway to Pakistan's dynamic industrial landscape, bringing together a powerhouse of manufacturers, exporters, and innovators under one high-impact platform. This strategic exhibition is meticulously designed to accelerate business visibility, global networking, and the formation of high-value industrial partnerships.

The event features a comprehensive showcase of Faisalabad’s economic diversity, from the world-class Textile and IT Pavilions to specialized zones for Real Estate and massive industrial developments. It is an unparalleled ecosystem dedicated to highlighting the entrepreneurial energy and resilient growth of the region's core sectors.

KEY SECTORS OF FAISALABAD ECONOMY

Faisalabad's economy thrives on diverse sectors that drive innovation, export growth, and economic resilience across multiple industries.

TEXTILE & APPAREL

Core export-driven sector covering complete value chain from yarn to finished garments.

CHEMICAL & PHARMACEUTICALS

Produces essential industrial chemicals and healthcare-related products.

MANUFACTURING & ENGINEERING

Supports industrial production through machinery, auto parts, and engineering solutions.

RETAIL & ECOMMERCE

An emerging sector focused on modern trade, online marketplaces, and consumer-driven business models.

EDUCATION & SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Institutions and training centers that develop skilled labor, technical expertise, and innovation capacity.

BANKS & FINANCIAL MARKETS

Provide financial services, investment support, and capital access to facilitate business growth.

FOOD & CONFECTIONERY

Confectioneries and food processing businesses that make and sell sweets, chocolates, and treats.

SOLAR & GREEN ENERGY SOLUTIONS

Harness renewable sources like sunlight, wind, and biomass to generate clean, sustainable power.

VEHICLE MANUFACTURERS

Automotives are vehicles and technologies related to cars, trucks, and transportation solutions.

AGRO INDUSTRY

Provides raw materials and supports food processing and agro-based manufacturing.

IT & INNOVATIONS

Drives digital transformation through software, startups, and tech-enabled services.

REAL ESTATE

High-value urban and industrial developments that support long-term economic expansion.

WHY FAISALABAD

Faisalabad is widely recognized as the textile hub of Pakistan, contributing approximately 30%–40% of the country’s textile exports and hosting thousands of industrial units.

Additionally:

  • Over 8,500 textile-related firms operate in the city
  • It plays a central role in global textile supply chains
  • Known as the “Manchester of Pakistan” due to its industrial scale
