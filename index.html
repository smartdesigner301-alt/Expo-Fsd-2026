ABOUT EXPO FAISALABAD

Expo Faisalabad 2026 stands as the premier gateway to Pakistan's dynamic industrial landscape, bringing together a powerhouse of manufacturers, exporters, and innovators under one high-impact platform. This strategic exhibition is meticulously designed to accelerate business visibility, global networking, and the formation of high-value industrial partnerships.

The event features a comprehensive showcase of Faisalabad’s economic diversity, from the world-class Textile and IT Pavilions to specialized zones for Real Estate and massive industrial developments. It is an unparalleled ecosystem dedicated to highlighting the entrepreneurial energy and resilient growth of the region's core sectors.

WHY FAISALABAD

Faisalabad is widely recognized as the textile hub of Pakistan, contributing approximately 30%–40% of the country’s textile exports and hosting thousands of industrial units.

Additionally:

  • Over 8,500 textile-related firms operate in the city
  • It plays a central role in global textile supply chains
  • Known as the “Manchester of Pakistan” due to its industrial scale
